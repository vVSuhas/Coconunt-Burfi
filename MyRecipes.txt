
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups coconut powder(desiccated
coconut)
1/2 tin condensed milk(milkmaid)
a few drops pink color
a silver sheet(varak),optional

Method:
1.In a flat dish spread coconut powder
and microwave for 2 minutes to dry
roast the coconut.
2.Add milkmaid. Mix well. Divide into two
parts.To one part add pink color.
3.Spread white part in the tin.Top with
the pink part
4.Decorate with the silver sheet. Refrigerate
for 1 hour. Cut into pieces for serving
